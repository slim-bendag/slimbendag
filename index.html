Summary

Msc.-Ing. Slim Bendag

Dedicated Doctoral Student and Aquaculture Engineer with a strong focus on sustainability. Currently, I'm engaged in designing and constructing an innovative shrimp farming system that integrates Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS) with multi-trophic aquaculture principles. My aim is to minimize water discharge while pioneering the use of eco-friendly materials as an alternative to plastic in system construction. Additionally, I'm working on the development of Black Soldier Fly-based feed formulations to enhance the sustainability of aquaculture practices.

PhD student

Jan 2022 - Now

The subject of my doctoral studies is "Sustainable recirculating shrimp aquaculture" my main tasks are as follows:
  • Design and construct a new concept of marine aquaculture systems that overcomes the limitations of sustainabilty in traditional Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS)
  • Investigate and source potential sustainable materials and utilize them in the construction of the system
  • Design a smart KNX based operation and control system to optimize the energy (human and electrical) usage and keep a better control over the system's parameters
  • Enhance biomass and species diversity limitations of traditional systems and include a variety of different species in the system with commercial and biological value (IMTA hybrid with aquaponics, Echinodermata, Mollusks, Algae, etc..)
  • Be involved in the developement of insect-based feed for shrimp farming
